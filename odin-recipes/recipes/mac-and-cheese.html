Mac and Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

Pasta

  • 8 oz (225 g) elbow macaroni (or any short pasta)
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt (for boiling water)

Cheese Sauce

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (480 ml) milk (whole or 2%)
  • 2 cups (200 g) sharp cheddar, freshly grated
  • 1/2 cup (50 g) Gruyère or mozzarella, grated (optional blend)
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp mustard powder or 1 tsp Dijon (optional, boosts “cheesy” flavor)

Optional Crunchy Topping (for baked version)

  • 3/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 tbsp butter, melted
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan
  • Pinch of paprika

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil, add salt, and cook pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook for 1–2 minutes to form a roux.
  3. Slowly whisk in milk, stirring constantly, until smooth and slightly thickened (about 3–4 minutes).
  4. Reduce heat to low. Stir in shredded cheese until melted and creamy.
  5. Season sauce with salt, pepper, and optional spices (garlic powder, mustard, paprika).
  6. Add the drained pasta into the cheese sauce and stir until fully coated.
  7. (Optional) Transfer to a baking dish, sprinkle with breadcrumb topping, and bake at 400°F (205°C) for 12–15 minutes until golden.
  8. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
