Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, sourdough, or your choice)
  • 2 slices of cheese (American, cheddar, or your favorite melting cheese)
  • 1–2 tablespoons butter (softened)
  • (Optional) 1 slice tomato or cooked bacon for extra flavor

Steps:

  1. Spread butter evenly on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Layer cheese slices on top of the bread in the skillet.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.
  5. Cook until the bottom slice is golden brown and the cheese begins to melt, about 3–4 minutes.
  6. Carefully flip the sandwich and cook the other side until golden brown and the cheese is fully melted, about 2–3 minutes more.
  7. Remove from skillet, let cool for 1–2 minutes, then slice and serve warm.
